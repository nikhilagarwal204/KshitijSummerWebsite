Kshitij also called KTJ, is the annual techno-management festival of IIT Kharagpur. Kshitij developed exponentially, it gets an overpowering response from the students from various famous technical institutes and educational communities everywhere throughout the world. Everyone is getting attracted toward Kshitij and are actively participating in its events creating a more competitive, challenging and developing environment.
